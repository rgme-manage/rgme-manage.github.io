Johan Cristobal, PhD

Visiting Assistant Professor, Loyola Marymount University

RGME Interest: Teaching and Pedagogy in Graduate Mathematics Education. Cristobal is interested in the development of mathematics graduate students as instructors (and future professors). His dissertation focused on first time mathematics graduate student instructors (MGSIs) as they taught as the main instructor for the first time. Using frames, he understood how MGSIs framed their teaching and their students' learning, and how that spoke towards the culture of mathematics at the undergraduate-level. Feel free to contact me if you're interested in chatting about MGSIs or doing a Math Ed dissertation in a math PhD program!
