RESEARCH in GRADUATE MATHEMATICS EDUCATION

We envision this group as becoming a space for formal research venues (or “homes”) for the rising significant interest in graduate mathematics education from both practitioners and researchers.

We aim to provide space for researchers, leaders in graduate education, graduate mathematics faculty, and graduate students to convene and work collaboratively on research-based solutions to improving graduate mathematics education.
