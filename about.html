About the Group

In our community, we use Research in Graduate Mathematics Education (RGME) to be inclusive of all scholarship and sustained inquiry (broadly construed) related to graduate mathematics education. This includes discipline-based educational research, scholarship of teaching and learning, and research-informed design efforts related to graduate education. For an overview of the wide range of topics and questions that fall under RGME, you can read about our highlighted Areas of Interest.

We use "graduate mathematics education" as an umbrella term to refer to graduate education in the mathematical sciences. Our definition of “graduate student” is also purposefully broad and includes mathematics education graduate students.

You do not need to be a researcher to be a part of our RGME community! This is a space for researchers, practitioners, teachers, graduate students, and all interested others to convene and work collaboratively on research-based solutions to improving graduate mathematics education.
